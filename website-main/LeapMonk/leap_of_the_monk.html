About This Game

Created for Trijam #211 (Theme: Insane Accuracy)

Game Overview

In this game you play as a monk and you try to jump across as many bamboo poles as possible in an attempt to be enlightened and reach Nirvana. The bamboo poles are extremely small and require insane accuracy to land on.

Features

  • Randomly Generated Levels
  • Engaging Difficulty
  • Immersive Atmosphere
